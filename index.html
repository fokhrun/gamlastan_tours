Experience the Beauty of Gamlastan

Discover the historical wonders of Gamlastan on our guided tours. It takes visitors on a journey through medieval Stockholm. The tour promises to provide an immersive experience of the city's history through sights, sounds, and flavors. It encourages people to step back in time and explore the narrow, cobblestone streets where lords, ladies, and shopkeepers once roamed. The Old Town is described as an ideal starting point for visitors to learn about Swedish culture and the city's fascinating past. The tour begins at Stortorget, the main square, and includes stops at the Royal Palace, where visitors can learn about Swedish royalty, and a sampling of traditional candy. The tour concludes on the island of Riddarholmen, offering beautiful views and the oldest building in Stockholm.

The Stockholm Old Town Walk is a recommended tour for those interested in experiencing the historical charm of the city. It provides an opportunity to travel back in time, learn about Swedish history, and explore notable landmarks. The tour guides visitors through Stortorget, the Royal Palace, and various historical sites, ending on Riddarholmen Island. It promises an enriching and enjoyable experience that captures the essence of medieval Stockholm.
